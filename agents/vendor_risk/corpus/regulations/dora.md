# DORA — ICT Third-Party Risk (summary)

## Scope
DORA (EU Digital Operational Resilience Act) applies to financial entities
and their ICT third-party service providers.

## Clauses
- **Art. 28**: Risk assessment of ICT third-party arrangements BEFORE
  contract conclusion.
- **Art. 30**: Mandatory contractual provisions including audit rights,
  security obligations, subcontracting controls, exit strategies.
- **Critical ICT providers**: subject to direct EU regulatory oversight.

## Requirements TPRM Must Verify
- The vendor's incident reporting timeline meets DORA Article 19 windows.
- Subcontracting is permitted only with controller approval (mirrors
  GDPR Art. 28).
- An exit strategy / data-portability clause exists in the contract.
