# FFIEC Appendix J — Strengthening the Resilience of Outsourced Technology Services

## Scope
Federal financial regulators' guidance on third-party tech services.

## Clauses
- **Due diligence**: financial condition, business continuity capacity,
  subcontractor management.
- **Contract requirements**: SLAs, audit rights, BCP requirements,
  termination assistance.
- **Concentration risk**: where one TPSP supports multiple critical
  functions, concentration must be assessed.

## TPRM Verification
- Business Continuity Plan (BCP) document required for HIGH-tier vendors.
- Subcontractor management — must mirror parent's controls.
