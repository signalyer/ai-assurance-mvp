# GLBA Safeguards Rule — Third-Party Oversight (summary)

## Clauses
- **16 CFR 314.4(f)**: Covered entities must oversee service providers by
  taking reasonable steps to select and retain those capable of maintaining
  appropriate safeguards.
- Written contract required.
- Periodic risk assessments.

## TPRM Verification
- Customer NPI (non-public personal information) handling: vendor must
  contractually commit to GLBA-equivalent safeguards.
- Concurrent GDPR + GLBA scope requires the stricter of the two on each
  control.
