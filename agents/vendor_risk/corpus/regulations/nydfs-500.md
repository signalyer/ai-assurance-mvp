# NYDFS Part 500 — Third-Party Service Provider Security Policy (§500.11)

## Clauses
- **§500.11(a)**: Covered entities must implement written policies and
  procedures for third-party service provider security.
- **§500.11(b)**: Due diligence required including review of policies,
  identification of access points, and minimum cybersecurity practices.
- **§500.11(b)(2)**: Contractual provisions requiring TPSP MFA, encryption
  in transit and at rest, breach notification.

## TPRM Verification
- Encryption: BOTH in-transit AND at-rest must be contractually required —
  ambiguous "encryption" claims fail §500.11(b)(2).
- MFA: contractually required for TPSP access to covered entity systems.
- Breach notification: 72 hours.
