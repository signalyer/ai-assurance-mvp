# GDPR Article 28 — Processor Obligations (summary)

## Clauses
1. **Processor selection (Art. 28(1))**: Controllers must use only processors
   providing sufficient guarantees of technical and organisational measures.
2. **Written contract (Art. 28(3))**: A DPA must bind the processor on
   subject-matter, duration, nature, purpose, type of personal data, and
   categories of data subject.
3. **Sub-processing (Art. 28(2))**: No subprocessor without prior written
   authorisation. The DPA must list approved subprocessors.
4. **Confidentiality (Art. 28(3)(b))**: Personnel must be under confidentiality.
5. **Security (Art. 28(3)(c))**: Implement Article 32 security measures.
6. **Audit rights (Art. 28(3)(h))**: Controller has audit and inspection
   rights against the processor.
7. **SCCs for transfers**: Cross-border transfers require the 2021 SCC module
   (Module 2: Controller → Processor) or an adequacy decision.

## Carve-Outs
Any subprocessor excluded from SCC obligations is a material concern.
GDPR does not permit selective carve-outs from the Article 32 security
floor.
